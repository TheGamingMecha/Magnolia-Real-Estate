About Us

About us

     We have been married 40+ years and have lived all of our married life in Alcorn County.  We are blessed with two grown children:  a daughter. Anna, and a son, Russ.  We now enjoy 3 grandchildren and 2 grand-dogs. 


     My wife, Sherri is an RN and has worked in nursing for many years. After receiving her master's degree in nursing, she taught nursing at Northeast MS Community College  19 years before retiring.


     I have worked 42 years in education teaching science at Kossuth, Alcorn Central, Itawamba Community College, Northeast MS Community College, and finally serving as principal at Kossuth High School. I have also served on the faculty of Union University, the University of North Alabama, and McNairy County schools.


      During our marriage, we have owned 6 different homes,  3 of which we built.  We have also owned several rental properties. We have owned a trophy business, a floral business and we were co-owners of a monument business. 


      We know our experience in serving customers in business and our real estate experience will enable us to serve you with exceptional service and dedication.  We hope to hear from you soon.


Randy
